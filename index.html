Groot

Groot is a character from the Marvel comics and movie 'Guardians of the Galaxy'. He is a Flora colossus who's only phrase in the movie is "I am Groot." Except for the occasional, "We are Groot." His partner in crime is Rocket Raccoon, and they serve as bounty hunters attempting to capture Starlord (Peter Quill). Although he doesn't say much, Groot is a charming character who is a strong member of the Guardians of the Galaxy.

Groot appears in the comics and film, 'Guardians of the Galaxy'.
